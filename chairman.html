Sri. Ch. Malla Reddy

Founder Chairman, Mallareddy University

About Sri. Ch. Malla Reddy

Position: Founder Chairman, Mallareddy University

About: Sri. Ch. Malla Reddy is a renowned educationist, visionary, and philanthropist who has dedicated his life to empowering students through high-quality education. As the founder of the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions, he has played a pivotal role in transforming the educational landscape in Telangana.

He is currently serving as the Hon’ble Minister of Labour and Employment, Government of Telangana. Under his leadership, the Mallareddy University was established to offer world-class academic and research opportunities to students across disciplines.

Contributions: With over two decades of experience in building successful institutions, he has been instrumental in promoting holistic education, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and student-centered learning. His efforts have earned wide recognition both in the academic and political arenas.

Vision: Sri. Ch. Malla Reddy envisions making Mallareddy University a hub of innovation, research, and global excellence where students are nurtured into socially responsible leaders and professionals.
