Deep Learning in Healthcare

Authors: Dr. A. Kumar, R. Mehta

Year: 2025

This groundbreaking publication explores the powerful intersection between deep learning technologies and modern healthcare solutions. Authored by leading researchers from Malla Reddy University, it offers insights into how artificial intelligence is transforming medical diagnostics, patient monitoring, drug discovery, and personalized medicine.

About the Book

"Deep Learning in Healthcare" delves into the architecture and implementation of AI systems designed for clinical applications. It bridges theoretical concepts with practical case studies to highlight how neural networks are leveraged to improve healthcare outcomes. The book is structured to cater to both academic researchers and healthcare professionals.

Key Highlights

  • Fundamentals of deep learning and neural networks
  • Medical image analysis using convolutional neural networks (CNNs)
  • Predictive modeling for patient outcomes
  • Ethics and bias in AI-powered healthcare
  • Integration of AI in hospital systems and electronic health records
  • Real-world applications and case studies
