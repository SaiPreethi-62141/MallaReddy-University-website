Academic & Professional Background

Dr. Arun Singh Chouhan is a seasoned academician and researcher with 15 years of teaching experience. He completed his Ph.D. in Computer Vision from Jodhpur National University after earning his B.E. and M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering. Prior to joining MallaReddy University, he held key roles including HOD and Associate Professor at Aurora’s Engineering College, Hyderabad. At MRU, he actively mentors postgraduate students and leads interdisciplinary tech projects focused on innovation and real-world impact.

Research & Expertise Areas

Dr. Chouhan’s core research interests include Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. His work bridges the gap between advanced theory and applied innovation, with a focus on computer vision systems and scalable AI architectures. He frequently collaborates with peers across departments and is committed to nurturing next-generation tech talent.
