Course Curriculum

MallaReddy University | B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

Curriculum Overview

Semester 1

Courses include Engineering Mathematics I, Physics, Programming in C, Engineering Drawing, and Professional Communication.

Semester 2

Topics include Data Structures, Digital Logic Design, Engineering Mathematics II, Object-Oriented Programming in Java.

Semester 3

Courses include DBMS, Computer Organization, Operating Systems, Software Engineering, and Data Structures Lab.

Semester 4

Includes Design & Analysis of Algorithms, Web Technologies, Computer Networks, Mini Project I, and open electives.

Semester 5

Specialization tracks begin: AI, Data Science, Cyber Security. Also includes Compiler Design, Cloud Computing, and Major Project I.

Semester 6

Advanced Electives, Research Methodology, Internship Preparation, and Innovation Lab Project are key components.

Semester 7

Capstone project phase begins, along with Entrepreneurship, Ethics in Technology, and Technical Writing.

Semester 8

Full semester Industry Internship or Research Project with a focus on real-world problem solving and publishing outcomes.
