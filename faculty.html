Meet Our Esteemed Faculty

Dr. Meeravali Shaikh
Head of Department – CSE, Wireless Networks & AI Specialist
With over 13 years of academic and research experience, Dr. Meeravali Shaikh heads the Computer Science & Engineering department at Mallareddy University. He holds a Ph.D. in CSE from Andhra University and has a strong foundation in Computer Networks, Wireless Sensor Networks, and Data Mining. Dr. Shaikh has guided numerous undergraduate and postgraduate projects and actively contributes to the curriculum innovation and research development within the department. His work focuses on the integration of AI and ML into communication systems, and he has been instrumental in fostering research-driven learning environments at the university.

Dr. P. Rambabu
Professor – Remote Sensing & GIS Specialist
With over 20 years of industry experience and 13 years in academia, Dr. P. Rambabu is a senior faculty member at Mallareddy University. He holds a Ph.D. in Remote Sensing, with earlier degrees in Civil Engineering and Geospatial Technologies. His career bridges both practical field applications and academic excellence. Dr. Rambabu specializes in GIS mapping, satellite data interpretation, and terrain evaluation techniques, and actively guides student research projects focused on environmental and infrastructure planning. His work connects engineering applications with geospatial science, supporting sustainable development and precision mapping strategies.
View Dr. P. Rambabu
Dr. Arun Singh Chouhan
Associate Professor – Computer Vision, AI, and Cloud Computing Expert
With 15 years of academic experience, Dr. Arun Singh Chouhan is a senior faculty member in the CSE department at Malla Reddy University. He earned his Ph.D. in Computer Vision from Jodhpur National University and has a strong background in artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and machine learning. Dr. Chouhan has previously served as HOD at Aurora’s Engineering College, Hyderabad, and currently guides postgraduate research while fostering industry-academic collaborations in intelligent systems and scalable AI solutions.
