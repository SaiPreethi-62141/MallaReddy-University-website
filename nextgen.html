Next-Gen Cybersecurity Models

Authors: S. Roy, Dr. N. Iyer

Year: 2025

"Next-Gen Cybersecurity Models" is an in-depth exploration into the evolving landscape of digital security. With increasing threats in the modern cyber ecosystem, this publication introduces intelligent defense frameworks, adaptive learning systems, and decentralized security strategies for next-generation networks and systems.

About the Book

This book bridges the gap between theoretical computer security and practical implementation. It provides a comprehensive analysis of cutting-edge cybersecurity techniques, including AI-driven threat detection, quantum-resistant algorithms, blockchain-enabled security, and zero-trust architectures. It is an essential resource for security analysts, software engineers, and graduate students.

Key Highlights

  • Evolution of cybersecurity: from traditional firewalls to autonomous agents
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in threat modeling
  • Quantum-safe cryptography and encryption protocols
  • Blockchain for identity verification and data integrity
  • Zero Trust Networks and access control models
  • Case studies on cybersecurity in IoT, healthcare, and financial sectors

Real-World Applications

The book outlines practical implementations and industry use-cases:

  • Building secure IoT ecosystems with lightweight encryption
  • Using AI to detect insider threats and malware
  • Smart contract vulnerabilities and blockchain mitigation strategies
  • Cybersecurity in autonomous vehicles and smart cities
  • Protecting critical infrastructure through predictive analytics

Who Should Read This Book?

This publication is designed for:

  • University students pursuing cybersecurity and computer science
  • Professionals in IT security and network administration
  • Policy makers focused on digital safety regulations
  • Startups building secure technology products
