Blockchain Voting System Project

Malla Reddy University - Department of Computer Science & Engineering

Overview

The Blockchain Voting System is a cutting-edge student project developed by final-year CSE students at Malla Reddy University. This prototype demonstrates a highly secure, transparent, and tamper-proof method of conducting elections using blockchain technology, ensuring trust and integrity in the democratic process.

Objective

The project aims to eliminate election fraud, ensure voter anonymity, and increase accessibility and reliability in electoral processes. By leveraging the decentralized and immutable nature of blockchain, this system enables a secure platform for casting and verifying votes digitally.

Features

  • End-to-end vote encryption and secure digital identity verification
  • Immutable ledger storing each vote as a blockchain transaction
  • Decentralized peer-to-peer network to prevent data tampering
  • Voter anonymity and transparency of results
  • Real-time results and auditability
  • User-friendly interface for voters and administrators

Technologies Used

  • Blockchain Platform: Ethereum (Solidity Smart Contracts)
  • Frontend: React.js, HTML5, CSS3
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Smart Contract Deployment: Truffle, Ganache
  • Wallet Integration: MetaMask
  • Database: IPFS and MongoDB for metadata

Benefits

  • Prevents vote manipulation and duplication
  • Increases voter turnout with remote access
  • Enhances election security and confidence in results
  • Ensures transparency without compromising privacy
  • Reduces costs and logistics of traditional elections

Use Cases

  • University student council elections
  • Corporate board voting and decision-making
  • Local body and municipal elections
  • NGO and cooperative society polling

Future Scope

The project can be expanded for national-level elections using consortium blockchains with biometric integration. Additional security features like zero-knowledge proofs, AI-based fraud detection, and multilingual interfaces can further increase usability. Integration with Aadhaar and digital ID platforms is also being explored.
