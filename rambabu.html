Academic & Professional Background

Dr. P. Rambabu is a seasoned academic and industry expert with a strong foundation in civil engineering and remote sensing. He completed his B.Tech in Civil Engineering, followed by M.Tech and Ph.D. in Remote Sensing. With over two decades of industry experience, Dr. Rambabu transitioned into academia where he has spent 13 years imparting knowledge and leading research. At Mallareddy University, he serves as a Professor in the Department of CSE, leveraging his interdisciplinary experience to bridge practical applications and theoretical knowledge.

Research & Expertise Areas

His primary areas of expertise include Remote Sensing, Geospatial Technologies, GIS Mapping, and their application in civil and environmental systems. Dr. Rambabu has mentored numerous student projects, guided research initiatives in satellite data interpretation, and contributed to the development of sustainable solutions using spatial technologies.
